Who am I?

My name is Nestor Ramirez, I am a proactive person who likes challenges. I enjoy cultural diversity and I am always facinated by the weird and the strange. I enjoy to travel to fullfill this desire, the more different to my culture people are, the more I like them. I am very open minded and like to learn.
